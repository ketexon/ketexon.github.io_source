--- layout: poem title: Brave New Generation date_published: 2021-04-06 --- And on that wooden scaffold awaits Unfinished men of helpless minds Greeted by the arrogant gait Of malignant monsters of various kinds Who happed the chance to cross them. Greed and wind, sorrow and wrath Look down, beckon, and condemn Those dreading to fight the man of Gath, In the desolate desert plains of pain, Where lies the baby, waiting to be slain, Acutely watching with hopeful eyes, Only to be blinded by earthly demise.
